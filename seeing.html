Easy Steps to Protect Yourself from Conjunctivitis (Pink Eye)

Follow these simple steps to keep your eyes healthy and free from this irritating condition. Taking precautions can go a long way towards preventing conjunctivitis.


1. Frequent Handwashing: Make handwashing a habit! Clean hands can keep germs at bay and reduce the risk of conjunctivitis.

2. Hands Off Your Eyes: Avoid touching or rubbing your eyes, as this can introduce germs and irritants. Keep those hands away and let your eyes stay comfortable.

3. Skip Contaminated Waters: Steer clear of swimming in contaminated water. Keeping your eyes away from potentially harmful environments is a smart move.

4. Wear Protective glasses: Give your eyes an extra layer of defense by wearing protective glasses, especially in places where there's a higher risk of infection.

5. See a Doctor: If you notice any symptoms, don't delay. Contact a doctor as soon as possible. Early intervention can make a big difference in preventing the spread of conjunctivitis.
